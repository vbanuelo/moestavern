ABOUT US- Moe's Tavern is owned and operated by the one and only Moe Szyslak. Nestled right in the heart of the town of Springfield, Moe’s is the go-to spot for cold beer, hot gossip, and questionable karaoke. Moe’s isn’t fancy,but that’s the point. It’s the kind of place where locals gather to unwind, laugh, and occasionally prank call the owner. Between our legendary Duff Beer, our signature Flaming Moe drink, our finger-licking Dare Devil pickeld eggs, and the occasional brawl over the remote, Moe’s has become a second home for Springfield’s finest and weirdest characters.
